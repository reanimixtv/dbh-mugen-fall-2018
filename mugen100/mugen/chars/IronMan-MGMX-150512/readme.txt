Iron Man -MGMX- for WinMUGEN & 1.+ by Duracelleur - 15.05.07
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

MGMX = Megamix. During Avengers AOU's wave, I wanted to edit a full and complete Iron Man.
Same as the ReHyped characters I've edited (Ryu, Ken, Ryuuken).
MVC styled gameplay based on my ToSix project.
Original character by Magus : http://www.scruffydragon.com/downloads.php?cat_id=3&download_id=84
A bunch of new specials and hypers based on :
Kong's Iron Man & War Machine, ZVitor's Iron Man, Erradicator's Iron Man, Magus's Ultron...
Stance sprite by Graphicus : http://mugenguild.com/forum/topics/mvc-sprite-edits-153407.0.html
MVC3 Voice pack by MGMurrow : http://mgmurrow.mugen-infantry.net/add-ons
